1–7번 · 어휘 / 어법 / 발음

1
Vocabulary · 숙어3점
어휘
밑줄 친 단어(구)의 의미와 가장 비슷한 것은?
① The firefighters quickly put out the fire.
② She is no longer interested in the job.
③ Stress can cause serious health problems.
④ She showed great courage during the difficult times.
✦
핵심 포인트!

put out = 불을 끄다 (extinguish), no longer = 더 이상 ~않다 (not anymore), cause = 유발하다 (bring about), courage = 용기 (bravery).

이런 기본 어휘·숙어는 문맥 속에서 뜻을 파악하는 연습이 중요해. 특히 put out처럼 동사+전치사 조합은 따로 정리해두면 좋아!

📝 단어 정리
put out
불을 끄다 (= extinguish)
no longer
더 이상 ~않다 (= not ~ anymore)
courage
용기 (= bravery, boldness)

문법
다음 문장과 형식이 다른 것은?
His parents encouraged him to study abroad. → 5형식 (S + V + O + OC)
①
Difficulties make you a stronger person.
②
I heard someone knocking on the window.
③
The teacher allowed the students to leave early.
④
Vitamin C keeps our skin smooth and healthy.
⑤
She teaches foreign visitors Korean language and culture.
✦
핵심 포인트!

제시문 "encouraged him to study"는 5형식 (목적어 + to부정사 목적보어). ①②③도 5형식이야.

④ "She teaches foreign visitors Korean language and culture" → 목적어가 두 개(간접목적어+직접목적어)인 4형식이라 다른 형식이야.

📐 5형식 vs 4형식
5형식
S + V + O + OC (목적보어) → make you happy, allow him to go
4형식
S + V + IO + DO (간접·직접목적어) → teach him English

11번 · 접속사 that / whether

11
Grammar · 접속사4점
문법
다음 빈칸에 'it'를 쓸 수 없는 문장은?
①
Let me know ___ you will attend the presentation.
②
I am worried about ___ my wireless network is secure or not.
③
The big question is ___ alternative energy is ready for mass use.
④
It's no longer a question of ___ your business will make a profit or not.
✦
핵심 포인트!

① "Let me know whether/if you will attend" → 참석할지 아닐지 알려줘. 여기서는 '~인지 아닌지'의 의미가 필요하므로 it은 불가.

②③④는 문맥상 whether/that 둘 다 가능하거나 it이 의미상 쓰일 수 있어. 하지만 ①처럼 yes/no 의문이 담긴 절은 반드시 whether/if를 써야 해.

📐 that vs whether/if
that
사실을 전달할 때 → I know that he is kind.
whether / if
~인지 아닌지 → I don't know whether he will come.

[20–23] 세계의 Comfort Food

20
Reading · 내용 일치3점
독해
윗글의 내용과 일치하지 않는 것은?
Comfort food is food that makes you feel good when you are sad, angry, or stressed out. It can also make you think of happy moments from the past. It satisfies both the stomach and the heart.

Jessica from USA: My comfort food is chicken soup. When I was small and caught a cold, my father made me a bowl of chicken soup. It warmed my body and I slowly started to feel better.

Maria from Brazil: I love cassava chips the most. Once when I had a bad day at school, my best friend bought me a bag. The crisp sound of eating chips made me feel good.

Simon from France: I love madeleines. My grandmother always makes madeleines for me. People in France enjoy madeleines as an afternoon snack.
①
Each person has different comfort food.
②
Americans eat chicken soup when they have a cold.
③
Maria eats cassava chips as comfort food.
④
People in France like eating madeleines as an afternoon snack.
⑤
Simon eats only madeleines that his grandmother made.
✦
핵심 포인트!

⑤ Simon은 할머니가 만든 것만 먹는다고 하지 않았어. 지문에는 "People in France enjoy madeleines as an afternoon snack"이라고 나와 있어서 ④는 사실이야.

지문에 없거나 과장된 내용을 찾는 게 핵심! only, always, never 같은 극단적 표현이 들어간 선택지를 먼저 의심해봐.

[24–25] 초콜릿과 감정 조절

24
Grammar · 어법4점
문법
밑줄 친 ①~⑤ 중 어법상 알맞지 않은 것은?
My comfort food is chocolate. I usually eat it when I feel bad. Recently, I didn't do well on a test and got stressed out. Since I couldn't focus, I had my sister ①recommend a good brand of dark chocolate. Then I went to the store and bought some chocolate so that I could feel more relaxed. The sweetness of the chocolate made me ②feel much better instantly. To keep this good mood, I got myself ③finish all my homework quickly. This sweet treat always helps me ④to stay cheerful even during tough times. Eating chocolate truly ⑤makes me feel good.
①
recommend → 어법 검토 필요
②
feel
③
finish
④
to stay
⑤
makes
✦
핵심 포인트!

"I got myself to finish" → get + 목적어 + to부정사. ③ finish는 to finish로 써야 해!

① have + O + 동사원형(recommend) ✓
② make + O + 동사원형(feel) ✓
③ get + O + to부정사(to finish) ← 이게 포인트!
④ help + O + to부정사(to stay) ✓

📐 사역/준사역 동사 비교
make / have / let + O + 동사원형
make me feel, have her go
get + O + to부정사
get him to finish ← to 필수!
25
Reading · 요약4점
독해
윗글의 내용을 한 문장으로 요약할 때 (A)(B)에 들어갈 알맞은 것은?
The writer eats chocolate to (A) stress and maintain a (B) attitude even in difficult situations.
①
(A) increase – (B) serious
②
(A) avoid – (B) negative
③
(A) reduce – (B) positive
④
(A) ignore – (B) nervous
⑤
(A) face – (B) creative
✦
핵심 포인트!

글의 핵심: 초콜릿을 먹으면 스트레스가 줄어들고(reduce) 기분이 긍정적(positive)으로 바뀐다.

요약 문제는 지문 전체의 방향을 파악해야 해. 글에서 초콜릿이 기분을 좋게 하고 스트레스를 덜어준다고 했으니 (A)=reduce, (B)=positive!

서술형 1·2·3 · 영작 및 변환

서1
Writing · 문장 완성4점
서술형
Answer the question in one sentence.
Q: What happened when Maria started to eat the chips?
대윤 답안
Her stress suddenly disappeared.
✦
핵심 포인트!

Maria가 칩을 먹기 시작하자 스트레스가 갑자기 사라졌어. Her stress suddenly disappeared. ✓

서술형은 조건(주어+동사 포함, 제시 단어 활용)을 꼭 지켜야 해. 대윤이 완벽하게 썼어! 👏

서2
Writing · 영작10점
서술형
다음을 조건에 맞게 영어로 쓰시오. (pass / so / can 활용)
나는 이 시험에 합격하기 위해 열심히 공부했다.
대윤 답안
I study hard so that I can pass this exam.
✦
핵심 포인트!

so that + 주어 + can + 동사원형 = ~하기 위해서. 목적을 나타내는 부사절 구문이야!

"I study hard so that I can pass this exam." 완벽해! 👏

서2-2
Writing · 사역동사6점
서술형
다음을 조건에 맞게 영어로 쓰시오. (have / clean 활용)
나는 남동생에게 내 방을 청소하게 시켰다.
대윤 답안
I had my brother clean my room.
✦
핵심 포인트!

사역동사 have + 목적어 + 동사원형. "I had my brother clean my room." — clean은 to 없이 동사원형으로 ✓

📐 사역동사 have
have + O + 동사원형
I had him fix the car. → 그에게 차를 고치게 했다
서3
Writing · 문장 변환8점
서술형
다음을 같은 의미의 다른 표현으로 바꾸시오.
(1) The room is full of people.
(2) She invited her friends as well as her family.
대윤 답안 (1)
The room is filled with people.
대윤 답안 (2)
She invited not only her family but also her friends.
✦
핵심 포인트!

(1) be full of = be filled with (수동태) 둘 다 '~로 가득 차다' ✓

(2) B as well as A = not only A but also B — 어순 주의! A와 B 위치가 바뀌어. 대윤이 정확하게 썼어! 👏

📐 중요 동의 표현
be full of = be filled with
full of hope = filled with hope
B as well as A = not only A but also B
friends as well as family = not only family but also friends
